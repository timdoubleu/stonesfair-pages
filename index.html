Stonesfair inspires confidence

Stonesfair inspires confidence when we throw “our hat into the ring” because we do what we say we will do. Stonesfair has substantial financial resources available to close acquisitions quickly when an opportunity arises. Unlike institutional and REIT investment firms, Stonesfair has only two decision-makers who decide which properties to acquire, determine which lender to move forward with on an acquisition, and develop and implement the structure and pro-forma of the investment. Therefore, Stonesfair is able to move quickly in any purchase transaction by providing confidence to both the Seller of a property and the resulting Lender of the investment acquisition.

In acquiring a new property, Stonesfair’s goal is to “make money the day escrow closes”. This is mainly the result of our thorough due diligence process, which is widely applauded by brokers, lenders, and investors. Our team of professionals are capable of conducting due diligence in as little as 10 days. Each potential investment property is carefully reviewed and put to the test of Stonesfair’s rigid acquisition criteria in order to ensure its long term success. We pride ourselves on extensive due diligence, but seek elevated returns through an investment strategy that can react to changing financial times and markets.

About Us

Stonesfair is a private real estate investment and management firm located in the San Francisco Bay Area. Stonesfair specializes in the pursuit, acquisition, financing, and managing of apartment communities in distinct target markets. We specifically target multi-family properties within a 2 to 2.5 hour travel time from our corporate office.

This ensures frequent visits to the properties to monitor operations and keeping a keen eye on the look and feel of a property. We operate multifamily real estate with goals of cash flow, appreciation, and protecting and preserving investor principal. Stonesfair and its staff are dedicated to provide its residents with excellent service and a high quality living environment
